# Executive Insights Examples

## Key Overspend Drivers
Cloud and external labor are the primary pressure points over the 5-year period. Azure and AWS spend accelerates as data engineering, analytics, and cloud platform teams expand usage.

## Cost Center Risks
Cost centers 104018, 104023, 104026, and 109003 show recurring variance pressure from cloud consumption, managed services, and project delivery resources.

## Cloud Spend Trend
Cloud spend grows materially from 2022 to 2026, especially in Infrastructure & Cloud and Enterprise Data Services. This should trigger optimization reviews, reserved capacity analysis, tagging governance, and chargeback accountability.

## External Labor Risk
External labor increases in 2025 and 2026 due to contractor ramp-up, delivery support, and managed services. Contract end date risk should be monitored monthly.

## Vendor Concentration
Microsoft Azure, AWS, Capgemini, ServiceNow, and Snowflake represent significant concentration. Leaders should review renewal timing, rate increases, and strategic dependency.

## Recommended Actions
1. Launch monthly cloud optimization review.
2. Review external labor by role, vendor, and end date.
3. Require project owners to validate forecast assumptions quarterly.
4. Track vendor renewal risk 180 days before renewal.
5. Use the dashboard as the monthly CIO operating review source of truth.
